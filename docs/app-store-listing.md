## Description (4000 chars max)

Gluco helps you understand how your meals, movement, and sleep connect to how you feel — without the overwhelm.

Snap a photo of your plate or scan a nutrition label, and Gluco uses AI to break down what you're eating. Log meals in seconds, track your energy and cravings with quick check-ins, and watch patterns emerge over time. Sync with Apple Health to bring your steps, sleep, and heart rate into one clear picture.

MEAL LOGGING MADE SIMPLE
- Snap a photo and let AI identify your meal and estimate nutrition
- Scan nutrition labels for instant macro breakdowns
- Search a database of thousands of foods
- Quick manual entry when you're on the go
- Save favorites and recent meals for one-tap logging

YOUR DAILY DASHBOARD
- See meals, activity, sleep, and wellness trends at a glance
- Metabolic Score tracks your overall wellness week by week
- Time range views: 7 days, 14 days, 30 days, or 90 days
- Quick post-meal check-ins to track energy, fullness, and cravings

APPLE HEALTH INTEGRATION
- Automatically sync steps, sleep, resting heart rate, and HRV
- See how your activity and sleep relate to how you feel after meals
- All your health data in one place — no manual entry needed

PERSONALIZED INSIGHTS
- AI-powered tips tailored to your patterns and goals
- Weekly summaries that highlight what's working
- Gentle nudges based on behavior science, not guilt
- Designed around CDC Diabetes Prevention Program principles

ACTIONS & CARE PATHWAYS
- Short-term actions: targeted 24-72 hour wellness goals
- 7-day structured plans to build lasting habits
- Choose your coaching intensity: light nudges, balanced, or structured
- Track progress with clear completion indicators

BUILT FOR YOU
- Set your own wellness goals (up to 3)
- Choose how you track: meals only, meals + Apple Health, or add personal readings
- Dietary preference and cultural food context support
- Pick your preferred daily nudge window

DESIGNED WITH CARE
- Gluco is a wellness tool, not a medical device
- Supportive language that encourages, never shames
- Your data stays private — we never sell it
- Built on evidence-based behavior change techniques

Start with a free account. Premium features available with an optional subscription.
